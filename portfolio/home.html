About Me

I am graduating in May of 2025 from the University of Massachusetts Amherst (UMass) with a bachelor of arts in English and a minor in education. Since the fall of 2024, I have been working as an intern for the School of Earth and Sustainability (SES) at UMass, working on projects related to a climate science training that SES is developing. I am passionate about environmentalism and environmental justice. I am interested in pursuing a career related to those interests and in which I can continue developing my writing, communication, and research skills.
